# Posey

**Your reading companion.**

Posey is a reading app for iPhone. Import documents in seven formats and read or listen in a clean, focused environment. Sentence-synchronized text-to-speech keeps your eyes and ears in sync. Notes and bookmarks stay anchored to their passages. Everything stays on your device.

---

## Features

**Seven formats, one experience**
PDF, EPUB, DOCX, MD, HTML, RTF, TXT. Import from Files, iCloud, or any app that supports sharing. Posey extracts the text, preserves the structure, and opens everything in a consistent reading environment. Scanned PDFs are handled with on-device OCR.

**Read and listen together**
Posey highlights each sentence as it's read aloud. Choose Best Available for your device's highest-quality voice, or Custom to pick any voice and set your own reading speed.

**Notes and bookmarks**
Annotate any passage. Annotations stay anchored to their position and appear in a unified view sorted by where they occur in the document.

**Navigate your documents**
Table of contents for structured documents. Full-text search. Images tab showing every image as a thumbnail with jump-to navigation. Reading progress indicator.

**Images inline**
Images, diagrams, and figures appear at their correct position in the text. Tap to view full screen. Pinch to zoom.

**No subscription. No account. No data collection.**
Posey is free. There are no servers, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

---

## Privacy

Posey does not collect any data. All reading activity is stored locally on your device. See the [privacy policy](https://markfriedlander.github.io/Posey/privacy.html) for details.
