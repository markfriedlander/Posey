Posey

A reading companion for iPhone.

Import a document. Read it. Listen to it. Take notes. Come back tomorrow and pick up exactly where you left off.

Seven formats

PDF, EPUB, DOCX, MD, HTML, RTF, TXT. Import from Files, iCloud, or anywhere that supports sharing. Posey extracts the text, preserves the structure, and opens it in a consistent reading environment.

Read and listen together

Sentence-synchronized text-to-speech. Each sentence highlights as it's read aloud. Choose your device's best voice or pick any other and set your own reading speed.

Notes and bookmarks

Annotate any passage. Notes and bookmarks stay anchored to their position and appear in a unified view, sorted by where they occur in the document.

Find your way

Table of contents for structured documents. Full-text search. Images tab showing every figure as a thumbnail. Reading-progress indicator so you always know how much is left.

Inline images

Figures and diagrams appear where they belong in the text. Tap to view full screen. Pinch to zoom. Scanned PDFs are handled with on-device OCR.

Yours, on your device

No subscription. No account. No analytics. No servers. Everything stays on the phone in your pocket. Free.
