## 4. Description
```
Posey

She knows where everything is.

Posey is a reading companion for iPhone. Import PDFs, EPUBs, Word files, Markdown, HTML, RTF, or plain text and read or listen in a clean, focused environment. Everything stays on your device.

Tap to read. Tap to listen. Tap a passage to take a note.

READ AND LISTEN TOGETHER
Posey highlights each sentence as it's read aloud, keeping your eyes and ears in sync. Commuting, walking, or too tired to hold a book — Posey reads with you. Choose Best Available for your device's highest-quality voice, or Custom to pick any voice and set your own reading speed.

SEVEN FORMATS, ONE EXPERIENCE
PDF, EPUB, DOCX, MD, HTML, RTF, TXT. Import from Files, iCloud, or any app that supports sharing. Posey extracts the text, preserves the structure, and opens everything in a consistent reading environment. Scanned PDFs are handled with on-device OCR — no internet connection required.

NOTES AND BOOKMARKS
Annotate any passage. Notes and bookmarks stay anchored to their position and appear in a unified view sorted by where they occur. Tap any annotation to return to the passage.

NAVIGATE YOUR DOCUMENTS
Jump to any chapter with the table of contents. Search for any word or phrase. Track your progress with a reading indicator so you always know where you are and how much is left.

IMAGES INLINE
Images, diagrams, and figures appear in the text where they belong. Tap to view full screen. Pinch to zoom.

NO SUBSCRIPTION. NO ACCOUNT. NO DATA COLLECTION.
Posey is free. There are no servers, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. Your documents and reading activity never leave your device.

She knows where everything is.
```

## 14. Privacy Questionnaire Answers

When App Store Connect asks:

- **Data Not Collected** — confirm. Posey does not:
  - Collect any user data
  - Use analytics or telemetry
  - Use advertising identifiers
  - Track users across apps or websites
  - Send any data to external servers operated by the developer
- **Third-party SDKs that collect data:** None. Apple frameworks only.
- All document processing, OCR, and text-to-speech occur entirely on device.
