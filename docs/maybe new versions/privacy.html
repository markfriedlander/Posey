Privacy

Posey does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data of any kind.

All of your reading activity — including imported documents, reading position, notes, bookmarks, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. Nothing leaves your device. There are no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no telemetry, no advertising identifiers, and no tracking of any kind.

The app does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or health data.

Document processing, text extraction, OCR for scanned PDFs, and text-to-speech all occur entirely on your device using Apple's built-in frameworks. No document content is ever transmitted anywhere.
