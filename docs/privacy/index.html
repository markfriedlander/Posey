Posey — Privacy Policy

Posey does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data of any kind.

All of your reading activity — including imported documents, reading position, notes, bookmarks, Ask Posey conversations, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. Nothing leaves your device. There are no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising of any kind.

Ask Posey uses Apple Intelligence to answer questions about your documents. Most requests are processed entirely on your device. For more complex requests, Apple Intelligence may use Private Cloud Compute — Apple's privacy-preserving cloud architecture in which your data is used only to fulfill your request, is never stored, and is never accessible to Apple or anyone else. Independent security researchers can verify this promise at any time. Posey has no servers of its own and no visibility into any data processed by Apple Intelligence.

The app does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or health data.
