Posey — Support

What is Posey?

Posey is a reading companion for iOS. Import documents — PDFs, EPUBs, Word files, Markdown, HTML, RTF, and plain text — and read or listen in a clean, focused environment. Ask questions about what you're reading using Apple Intelligence. Take notes anchored to specific passages. Everything stays on your device.

Getting Started

Tap the import button to add a document from Files, iCloud, or any app that supports sharing. Posey extracts the text, indexes it, and opens it in the reader. Tap the play button to start listening. Tap any sentence to jump to that position.

Reading and Listening

Posey highlights each sentence as it's read aloud, keeping your eyes and ears in sync. Two voice modes are available in Preferences. Best Available uses your device's highest-quality voice with a fixed reading rate. Custom lets you choose any voice on your device and adjust the reading speed with a slider.

Ask Posey

Tap any passage you're curious about and select Ask Posey from the menu. Ask what it means, define a term in context, or find related passages elsewhere in the document. Answers are grounded in your document — Posey won't invent information that isn't there. Ask Posey works best with non-fiction: articles, essays, legal documents, academic papers, and reference material.

Notes and Bookmarks

Tap and hold any sentence to create a note or bookmark anchored to that position. All annotations appear in the Notes sheet, sorted by where they occur in the document. Tap any annotation to jump back to the passage.

Table of Contents

For documents with navigable structure — EPUBs, PDFs, Word files, Markdown, and RTF — the TOC button lists all sections. Tap any entry to jump there. Playback automatically skips past the table of contents when you start listening.

Search

Find any word or phrase in the current document. Navigate between matches with the arrows.

Troubleshooting

  • Voice not sounding right: Switch between Best Available and Custom in Preferences. Best Available requires downloading a high-quality voice in Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices.
  • Ask Posey says it can't find an answer: Tap a specific passage and ask from there rather than asking about the whole document. Specific, grounded questions work best.
  • Document looks wrong after import: Some PDFs are scanned images. Posey uses OCR to extract text from scanned pages, which may take a moment for long documents.
  • Import not appearing: Make sure you're sharing the file itself, not a link to it.
